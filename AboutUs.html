    In June 2021, Binalot sa Tondo launched as an internet business under the competent plans of Lyka Monreal. Despite initially encountering challenges and doubts, Lyka's continuous commitment to her work led the business ahead and helped it build its name and reputation in this industry.

    Within the Tondo area, the original culinary concept of Binalot sa Tondo soon captured the tastes of discriminating food connoisseurs. The restaurant gained acceptance for its innovative presentation and combination of classic flavors, making it a shining example of culinary excellence that attracted customers eager to try its special dishes.

    Having seen her business take off and the increasing demand for her products, Lyka Monreal decided to open an actual store in order to promote Binalot sa Tondo. This expansion was a major turning point in the restaurant's development since it made it possible for it to serve a wider range of customers and give them an engaging eating experience.

    The result of Lyka Monreal's entrepreneurial leadership and dedication to culinary style stays as customers continue to pour into Binalot sa Tondo to enjoy its wonderful dishes, guaranteeing that the restaurant will continue to be an example of delicious foods delight for years to come.
